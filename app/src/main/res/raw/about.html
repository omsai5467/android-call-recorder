About

Android friendly!

A call recorder with a custom recording folder option.

MOST PHONES DOES NOT SUPPORT CALL RECORDING. Blame Google or your phone manufacturer, not me!

If high quality (voice line) sound recording is not available, this app will switch to MIC recording. No ads, open-source, GPLv3 license.
